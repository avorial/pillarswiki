# Bloodlines Era and the New Promise Mandarinate

## Range

2003-2014

## Summary

The Camarilla, neo-Anarchs, Sabbat, and Kuei-jin fight over the city after the Bloodlines-era crisis. The NPM consolidates, manipulates treaty appearances, and eventually breaks what remains of the old Free State.

## Timeline Events

- 2003 - The Tower Installs LaCroix
- 2005 - LaCroix Falls to Treachery
- 2007 - The Edict of Succession
- 2011 - The NPM Wins the Free States War
- 2013 - The Free States Completely Fall
- 2014 - The Treaty Masquerade Breaks
